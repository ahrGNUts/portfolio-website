Frederick Gorlame is a world renowned writer known for his work on Dagger 7, Burying Billy Beige, and Sweetwater. He had published thirty-seven works of fiction prior to his debut in screenwriting and continues to write books in between writing for award-winning television series'.

Ever the accomplished linguist, Frederick has written many of his books in English and Spanish directly, without the use of a translator. English is his native tongue, and Spanish he fell in love with during his second year at Rutgers while he was learning Latin. It is his great lament that Latin has not withstood the test of time to remain a language commonly spoken, but he has decided to post an excerpt from his next release. Happy translating!
